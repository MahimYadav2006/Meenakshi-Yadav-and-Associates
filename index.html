About Meenakshi Yadav

With over two decades of legal expertise, Meenakshi Yadav is a distinguished advocate and legal consultant specializing in corporate law, intellectual property rights, and civil litigation. As the founding partner of Meenakshi Yadav & Associates, she has successfully handled numerous high-profile cases and provided strategic legal counsel to multinational corporations and startups alike. Her commitment to excellence and innovative approach to legal challenges has earned her recognition as one of the leading lawyers in the NCR region. Beyond her legal practice, Meenakshi is deeply committed to legal education and social justice, regularly conducting workshops and pro bono services through the Yashashish Foundation.

Yashashish Foundation

The Yashashish Foundation is committed to providing pro bono legal services and community support across the NCR region. Our mission is to ensure access to justice for all, particularly focusing on underprivileged communities. Through legal awareness workshops, free consultations, and educational initiatives, we strive to create a more equitable legal system. The foundation also provides scholarships to aspiring law students and supports various social justice causes.
